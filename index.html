How it works

Three simple steps, every day

Parents guide. Kids grow. Everyone sees the progress add up.

1
📝

Parents set the tasks

Pick from ready-made tasks or create your own — set points, categories, due dates, and whether it’s a must-do or a bonus.

2
📱

Kids complete & show proof

Kids log in with a PIN, see their tasks, tick them off, and snap a photo as proof when it’s needed.

You approve, points add up, streaks build, and goals get closer — small wins turning into strong habits.
